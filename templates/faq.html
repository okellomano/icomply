FAQ

Below are some of the most common questions and answers about the DPA.

The Data Protection Act, 2019 Any organization that processes the personal data of people in the Kenya must comply with the DPA. “Processing” is a broad term that covers just about anything you can do with data: collection, storage, transmission, analysis, etc. “Personal data” is any information that relates to a person, such as names, email addresses, IP addresses, eye color, political affiliation, and so on. The DPA is also not limited to for-profit companies.
