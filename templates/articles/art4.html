DPA - Sec. 4

Application

This Act applies to the processing of personal data —

  1. entered in a record, by or for a data controller or processor, by making use of automated or non-automated means:

    Provided that when the recorded personal data is processed by non-automated means, it forms a whole or part of a filing system;

  2. By a data controller or data processor who —
    1. Is established or ordinarily resident in Kenya and processes personal data while in Kenya; or
    2. Not established or ordinarily resident in Kenya, but processing personal data of data subjects located in Kenya.
