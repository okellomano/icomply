DPA - Sec. 7

Qualifications of Data Commissioner

  1. A person shall be qualified for appointment as the Data Commissioner if that person —
    1. holds a degree from a university recognized in Kenya in —
      1. Data science;
      2. Law;
      3. Information technology; or
      4. Any other related field;
    2. Has knowledge and relevant experience of not less than ten years;
    3. Meets the requirements of Chapter Six of the Constitution; and
    4. Holds a master's degree.
  2. The Data Commissioner shall be appointed for a single term of six years and shall not be eligible for a re-appointment.
