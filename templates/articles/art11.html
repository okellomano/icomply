DPA - Sec. 11

Vacancy in the Office of the Data Commissioner.

  1. The Office of the Data Commissioner shall become vacant, if the Data Commissioner—
    1. Dies;
    2. Resigns from office by notice in writing addressed to the President;
    3. Is convicted of an offence and sentenced to imprisonment for a term exceeding six months without the option of a fine;
    4. Is removed from office on the grounds of—
      1. Inability to perform the functions of office arising from mental or physical infirmity;
      2. Non-compliance with Chapter Six of the Constitution;
      3. Bankruptcy;
      4. Incompetence; or
      5. Gross misconduct.
  2. The Data Commissioner may enter into association with other bodies or organisations within and outside Kenya as appropriate in furtherance of the object of this Act.
