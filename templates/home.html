DPA 2021 Regulations

Some of the DPA regulations are as follows

Data Privacy

Appropriate safeguards for the protection of the privacy of the data subject should be provided by the data controller or processor.

Data Encryption

Personal data should be encrypted during transmission or at rest.

Data Minimization

Data controller should limit the collection of personal information to what is directly relevant and necessary.

Data Protection Policy

The regulation seeks to protect privacy of data subjects by enforcing responsible processing of personal data.

Obligations of a Data Processor

A data processor determines the purpose for which and the means by which personal data is processed.

Data Protection by Design / Default

Embedding data privacy features and data privacy enhancing technologies directly into the design of projects at an early stage.

Retention of personal data

Personal data should be retained for no longer than is necessary for the purposes for which it is meant.

Data Portability request

Data subjects may apply to port or copy their personal data from one data controller or data processor to another.
